How We Help Children & Families

Tap any section below to learn the specific ways HTI changes lives in our community:

Elementary & Middle School Students

+

What We Provide:

  • Chromebooks with ChromeOS Flex (fast, secure, kid-friendly)
  • Access to Google Classroom and educational apps
  • Internet safety guidance through video tutorials
  • Microsoft Office skills training

Real Impact: Kids who've never had a computer at home can now complete homework, participate in online learning, and catch up to grade level. For families sharing 1-2 devices among 5-6 people, our Chromebooks end the competition for screen time.

High School & College Students

+

Success Stories Include:

  • DeAndra Jones' daughter: Graduated high school while working part-time, now pursuing Business Administration degree in college
  • College diplomas and trade school certificates earned by HTI recipients
  • Teen mothers earning GEDs while raising children
  • CNA, Phlebotomist, and Medical Assistant certifications achieved

Support We Provide: Resume writing tools, college application access, online coursework capabilities, job search resources.

Children with Special Needs

+

Specialized Technology Support:

  • Xavier Williams (Autism): Uses Chromebook for online learning, homework, ABA therapy sessions, and therapeutic work
  • Shawn Burton's child: Special needs student now excelling in classroom with dedicated device
  • Customized learning tools and assistive technology
  • Flexibility for therapeutic and educational needs

Skills Learned: Internet navigation, Microsoft Office, email, online tutoring platformsall adapted for individual learning needs.

Families Facing Challenges

+

We Serve Families Experiencing:

  • Poverty: Single mothers and families in HUBZone communities
  • Homelessness: Through partners like Passage Home and Salvation Army
  • Addiction Recovery: Michael Hines overcame addiction, now works as loan officer from home
  • Unemployment: Octavia Jones secured IT Support role at Cox Communications after receiving Chromebook
  • Digital Inequality: Families with 5-6 people sharing 1-2 devices

Whole-Family Impact: When we equip a family, children complete homework, parents apply for jobs, and entire households connect to opportunity. Education and employment doors open for everyone.

Digital Literacy Training (156+ Hours)

+

Self-Guided Video Library:

  • "How To Use ChromeOS Chromebook" (1 minute)
  • "Got A New Chromebook? 10 Things You Need To Know" (13 minutes)
  • "Chromebook Basics for Seniors" (60 minutes)

Skills Recipients Learn:

  • Google Drive and cloud storage
  • Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel)
  • Email and internet navigation
  • Online security basics
  • Job application and resume tools

Ongoing Support: Tech support available through HTI team. Recipients report gaining confidence and independence in technology use.
